# S1 E21: More Than Disabilities with Tara Geraghty-Ellis

In this powerful conversation, author and disability advocate Tara Geraghty-Ellis discusses defying expectations and living an empowered life with multiple disabilities. Through sharing her journey from being told to expect limitations to building a successful career and marriage, Tara demonstrates how people with disabilities can thrive with proper support and self-advocacy.

## Episode Highlights

- Understanding visible and invisible disabilities
- Navigating education systems and medical care
- The importance of family advocacy and support
- Building independence despite expectations
- Finding love and partnership with disabilities
- Creating resources for the disability community
- The power of self-advocacy
- Breaking down stereotypes and barriers
- Building community support systems
- The importance of representation

## Key Takeaways

- Disability doesn't define capability
- Support systems are crucial for success
- Self-advocacy is essential
- Independence is possible with proper support
- Both visible and invisible disabilities present challenges
- Integration and inclusion matter
- Relationships and careers are achievable
- Community building creates change
