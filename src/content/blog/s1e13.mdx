# S1 E13: Overcoming Obstacles with Lisa Santiago McNeill

In this powerful episode, Lisa Santiago McNeill shares insights on moving past excuses and embracing our truth. As a publisher and mindset coach, she helps people tell their stories authentically while breaking free from self-imposed limitations. Through her own journey of publishing her story and building multiple successful ventures, Lisa demonstrates how confronting our truth can lead to transformation.

## Episode Highlights

- Understanding how excuses hold us back from our potential
- The importance of telling our truth from our own perspective
- Moving from victimhood to empowerment
- Building multiple successful ventures while staying authentic
- Balancing spirituality with authenticity
- The power of accepting ourselves fully
- Strategies for overcoming fear of success and failure
- The importance of not attaching to outcomes

## Key Takeaways

- Your truth is yours to tell from your perspective
- Excuses often mask areas where we need to take responsibility
- Success doesn't require conforming to others' expectations
- Everyone's journey and timeline are different
- Fear doesn't have to stop us - we can act despite it
- Our past experiences shape but don't define us
- Being authentic allows others to do the same
