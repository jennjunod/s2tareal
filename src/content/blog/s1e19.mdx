# S1 E19: Transformation and Transgender with Jack Ori

In this insightful conversation, writer and coach Jack Ori shares perspectives on transformation through both his personal journey as a transgender man and his work supporting parents of transgender youth. Through discussing his novel "Reinventing Hannah" and his coaching practice, Jack demonstrates how transformation is a universal experience that connects us all.

## Episode Highlights

- Understanding gender identity and transition
- Supporting transgender youth and their families
- The writing process and character development
- Navigating social and medical transition
- Building supportive family relationships
- The importance of safe self-expression
- Tips for coming out and finding support
- Parent education and resources
- Breaking down stereotypes
- The power of authentic storytelling

## Key Takeaways

- Transformation is a universal human experience
- Parents need support during their child's transition
- Coming out is a gradual, personal process
- Small acts of self-expression can be powerful
- Support systems are crucial for transition
- Good intentions count while learning
- Mistakes happen - correct and move forward
- Every journey is unique
