# S1 E5: Generational Trauma with Patience Modevi

In this powerful episode, mindset coach Patience Modevi shares her expertise on breaking generational trauma patterns and fostering cultural understanding. From her unique perspective as someone born in France to Togolese and Ghanaian parents, now living in London, she offers insights on helping young people develop confidence and self-worth while addressing broader systemic issues.

## Episode Highlights

- Understanding and breaking generational trauma patterns
- The impact of cultural identity on personal development
- Practical tools for building self-confidence and worth
- Creating positive change through individual growth
- The importance of cross-cultural understanding and empathy
- Strategies for supporting youth in their personal development

## Key Takeaways

- Breaking generational trauma starts with individual awareness and growth
- Cultural understanding comes from genuine community engagement
- Personal vision and role models are crucial for positive change
- Self-worth and confidence are foundational for breaking negative patterns
- Systemic change begins with individual transformation
