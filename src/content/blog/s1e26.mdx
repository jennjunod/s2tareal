# S1 E26: Money Management with Ean Price Murphy

Moxie Bookkeeping founder Ean Price Murphy discusses her path from personal bankruptcy to financial empowerment, sharing practical strategies for both personal and business money management while addressing the emotional aspects of financial decision-making.

## Episode Highlights

- Journey from bankruptcy to financial literacy
- Five common money myths debunked
- Practical cash management strategies
- Role of emotions in financial decisions
- Credit card management techniques 
- Personal vs business finances
- Starting point for new entrepreneurs
- Safe money management practices
- Building financial confidence
- Creating intentional spending habits

## Key Takeaways

- Financial literacy is a learnable skill
- You don't need complex budgets to manage money
- Math skills aren't required for good money management
- Safety concerns often underlie spending habits
- Start with 1% savings to build financial stability
- Pay credit cards weekly to maintain control
- Bookkeeping can be learned before outsourcing
- Community support aids financial growth
- Financial health requires emotional awareness
- Intentional spending beats strict control
