# S1 E8: Anger with Patrick Monette

In this insightful episode, licensed mental health counselor Patrick Monette breaks down different manifestations of anger - from passive to aggressive - while exploring how cultural and gender expectations influence our relationship with this powerful emotion. Through his work in both therapy and coaching, Patrick offers practical tools for understanding and managing anger in healthier ways.

## Episode Highlights

- Understanding different types of anger expression: passive, passive-aggressive, and aggressive
- The connection between suppressed emotions and physical/mental health
- Cultural impacts on emotional expression, especially for men
- The importance of finding the right therapeutic approach and support system
- How to create secure spaces for emotional exploration
- Practical tools for understanding and managing anger

## Key Takeaways

- Anger manifests differently in everyone - from passive to aggressive expressions
- Suppressed emotions often emerge in other ways - through anxiety, depression, or physical symptoms
- Finding the right support requires understanding what makes you feel secure
- Change doesn't mean losing yourself - it means becoming a healthier version of yourself
- Recovery and growth are ongoing journeys that require patience and self-compassion
