# S1 E25: Self Acceptance with Regard Tang

Empowerment coach Regard Tang shares her transformative journey from high-stress fashion industry makeup artist to mental health advocate and coach. Through exploring burnout, family relationships, and personal healing, she reveals how true self-acceptance requires more than surface-level self-care.

## Episode Highlights

- Transition from 10-year makeup career to empowerment coaching
- Physical and mental toll of fashion industry work
- Impact of COVID-19 on career and personal growth
- Healing childhood trauma through family reconnection
- Understanding burnout and toxic work environments
- Building foundations for genuine self-acceptance
- Role of therapy in personal transformation
- Importance of curiosity in overcoming judgment
- Creating unique identity beyond cultural boxes
- Value of support systems in healing

## Key Takeaways

- Self-acceptance requires deeper work than trendy self-care
- Burnout affects mental, physical, emotional and spiritual health
- High-functioning depression often goes unrecognized
- Support systems crucial for personal growth
- Healing requires facing uncomfortable truths
- Self-worth impacts relationship patterns
- Curiosity helps overcome judgment
- Identity doesn't need to fit predetermined boxes
- Change takes time and consistent effort
- Inner child work essential for adult healing
