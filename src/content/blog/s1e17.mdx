# S1 E17: Healing Trauma with Janine Wirth

In this powerful conversation, psychotherapist Janine Wirth shares insights on healing trauma through various therapeutic approaches, including clinical hypnotherapy. Through discussing her journey from trauma survivor to therapist, Janine offers perspective on empowerment, relationships, and breaking cycles of pain.

## Episode Highlights

- Understanding clinical hypnotherapy and its applications
- The difference between mothering and empowering
- Processing trauma through different therapeutic approaches
- Building healthy relationships after trauma
- The importance of choosing the right therapist
- Working with the subconscious mind
- Understanding different therapeutic modalities
- The role of self-awareness in healing
- Viewing triggers as opportunities for growth

## Key Takeaways

- Trauma exists on a spectrum and all experiences are valid
- Finding the right therapeutic approach is crucial
- Healing requires both compassion and accountability
- Triggers can be viewed as gifts for growth
- Everyone has experienced some form of trauma
- The right support comes at the right time
- Healing happens at your own pace
