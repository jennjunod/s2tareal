# S1 E28: Gut Health with Trish Whetstone 

Health coach Trish Whetstone discusses the complex relationship between gut health, mental wellness, and emotional eating. Through sharing personal experiences and professional insights, she demonstrates how mindful eating and understanding our body's signals can lead to better overall health.

## Episode Highlights

- Understanding inflammation and digestive health
- Role of gut microbiome in overall wellness
- Impact of processed foods on health
- Mindful eating practices and techniques
- Connection between mental and gut health
- Managing food sensitivities and cravings
- Building healthy relationships with food
- Emotional aspects of eating habits
- Importance of food awareness
- Journey toward balanced nutrition

## Key Takeaways

- Gut health affects physical and mental wellness
- Mindful eating supports better digestion
- Food choices impact microbiome health
- Processed foods can disrupt gut balance
- Emotional eating requires compassionate approach
- Body awareness aids healing journey
- Self-acceptance enhances health outcomes
- Small changes create lasting impact
- Individual nutrition needs vary
- Health journey is ongoing process
