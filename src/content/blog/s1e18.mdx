# S1 E18: Stereotypes with ALONZO

In this powerful conversation, recording artist and performer ALONZO discusses breaking free from societal boxes and embracing authentic self-expression. Through sharing his journey from dancer to singer and exploring identity beyond labels, ALONZO demonstrates how rejecting stereotypes can lead to personal freedom and meaningful art.

## Episode Highlights

- Breaking free from societal expectations and labels
- Navigating gender expression and stereotypes
- The journey from dance to music
- Understanding fear versus intuition
- Creating art with purpose and impact
- The importance of emotional authenticity
- Setting healthy boundaries
- Building supportive relationships
- Finding strength in vulnerability
- The power of self-acceptance

## Key Takeaways

- Authenticity requires rejecting societal boxes
- All emotions and expressions are valid
- Fear and intuition guide different choices
- Boundaries enable authentic relationships
- Self-care must come before giving to others
- Art can create meaningful social impact
- Vulnerability is strength
- Support systems ground and empower us
