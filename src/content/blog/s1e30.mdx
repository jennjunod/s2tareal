# S1 E30: Domestic Violence with Brittany Mcenteggart

Survivor and author Brittany Mcenteggart discusses her experiences with domestic violence, sexual assault, and family dysfunction, sharing how writing her autobiography helped transform trauma into advocacy. Now living in Ireland, she works to empower others through sharing her story of survival and resilience.

## Episode Highlights

- Journey through multiple abusive relationships
- Impact of childhood trauma and family dysfunction
- Process of escaping violent situations
- Moving to Ireland as fresh start
- Writing autobiography as healing tool
- Dealing with online criticism and mental health
- Finding support systems and stability
- Importance of sharing survival stories
- Understanding various forms of abuse
- Building resilience through adversity

## Key Takeaways

- Domestic violence includes multiple forms of abuse
- Escaping abuse requires immense strength
- Support systems crucial for healing
- Writing can aid trauma processing
- Advocacy helps transform pain to purpose
- Recovery is ongoing journey
- Self-worth develops through healing
- Sharing stories reduces shame
- Finding voice empowers others
- Resilience builds over time
