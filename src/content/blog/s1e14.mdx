# S1 E14: Trichotillomania with Tammi Vaughn

In this vulnerable conversation, holistic health and wellness coach Tammi Vaughn shares her four-decade journey with trichotillomania, a compulsive hair-pulling condition. Through discussing her path to healing childhood trauma and finding peace through nature, Tammi offers insights on self-awareness, meditation, and the power of facing our truth.

## Episode Highlights

- Understanding trichotillomania and its triggers
- Healing childhood trauma through inner child work
- The power of nature in healing and self-discovery
- Using meditation and self-awareness for transformation
- Creating safe spaces for vulnerability and growth
- The importance of addressing root causes
- Building a supportive community for healing

## Key Takeaways

- Healing begins with being truthful with ourselves
- Nature can be a powerful tool for self-discovery and healing
- Support systems should approach with grace and understanding
- Self-awareness is challenging but rewarding
- Meditation can help manage triggers and impulses
- Inner child work is crucial for healing trauma
- Creating safe spaces allows for authentic healing
