# S1 E6: Life After Divorce with Jasmine Rice

In this vulnerable episode, divorce coach Jasmine Rice shares her journey through divorce after a 23-year relationship that began in high school. She discusses the challenges of rediscovering yourself after divorce, dealing with shame and grief, and how she transformed her experience into a coaching practice helping others navigate their own healing journeys.

## Episode Highlights

- The complex journey from high school sweethearts to divorce after 14 years of marriage
- Navigating identity and self-discovery when you've never been alone
- The difference between therapy and coaching in divorce recovery
- Breaking through shame and learning self-forgiveness
- Finding yourself and your own interests post-divorce
- Turning personal pain into purpose through coaching others

## Key Takeaways

- Healing is not linear - it's okay to have good days and bad days
- Self-forgiveness is often harder than forgiving others
- Support looks different for everyone - sometimes just listening is enough
- Recovery takes time - it's a marathon, not a sprint
- Finding yourself after divorce is an opportunity for growth and discovery
