# S1 E9: Alcoholism with Kerri-Anne Kedziora

In this deeply moving episode, Kerri-Anne shares her journey from 27 years of alcoholism to six years of sobriety, alongside her husband Peter who supported her through recovery. Together, they discuss how their relationship helped break cycles of trauma and addiction, eventually leading them to build a successful business that now helps others find their own path to healing and success.

## Episode Highlights

- Kerri-Anne's journey through 27 years of alcoholism to recovery
- How childhood trauma influenced patterns of addiction
- The power of unconditional love and support in recovery
- Transforming personal struggles into business success
- Building a company culture focused on empowering others
- Finding purpose through helping others heal and grow

## Key Takeaways

- Recovery is possible with the right support system
- Healing from trauma is an ongoing journey
- Love and acceptance can break generational cycles
- Business can be a vehicle for helping others grow
- It's never too late to start over and build something new
