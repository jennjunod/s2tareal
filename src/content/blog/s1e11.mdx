# S1 E12: Improving Your Mindset with Paul Wilson

In this vulnerable and enlightening episode, mindset coach Paul Wilson opens up about his journey from working at the Channel Tunnel to becoming a hypnotist and healer. Through sharing his personal experiences with depression, career transitions, and finding purpose, Paul demonstrates how changing your mindset can transform your life.

## Episode Highlights

- Paul's 25-year career at the Channel Tunnel and his decision to make a change
- Journey into hypnotherapy and mindset coaching
- Personal experiences with depression and mental health
- The importance of seeking help and breaking mental health stigma
- Understanding the difference between situational challenges and mental illness
- Tools and techniques for managing mental health
- The courage it takes to be vulnerable and ask for help
- Making decisions that lead to positive life changes

## Key Takeaways

- Changing your mindset starts with making a decision and taking action
- There's no shame in seeking help or taking medication for mental health
- Being vulnerable takes courage, especially for men
- Life is temporary - don't wait to make positive changes
- Happiness comes from within, not from external sources
- It's never too late to change careers or follow your passion
- Finding someone to talk to is crucial for mental health
